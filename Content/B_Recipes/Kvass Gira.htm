Kvass/Gira

Lithuanian beverage made of bread.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 l water

  2. 100 g rye bread, dried and toasted (lithuanian bread is strongly recommended)

  3. 50 g sugar

  4. 1 teaspoon yeast

Instructions:

Prepare the bread: Cut the rye bread into small pieces and dry them in a toaster oven or a regular oven until they are crispy.

Boil the water: Bring the water to a boil in a large pot.

Add the bread: Add the dried bread to the boiling water and let it steep for 15-20 minutes.

Cool and strain: Remove the pot from the heat and let it cool completely. Strain the liquid to remove the bread pieces.

Add sugar and yeast: Add sugar and yeast to the cooled liquid. Stir well to dissolve the sugar.

Fermentation: Transfer the mixture to a clean, sterilized container. Cover the container with a cloth or paper towel and secure it with a rubber band. Let it ferment at room temperature for 1-2 days, or until it becomes bubbly and slightly acidic.

Bottle and store: Once the fermentation is complete, strain the kvass to remove any sediment. Bottle the kvass and store it in the refrigerator.
