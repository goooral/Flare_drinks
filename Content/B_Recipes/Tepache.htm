Tepache

A Mexican fermented pineapple drink.

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pineapple, unpeeled, cored, and cut into chunks

  2. 250 ml water

  3. 100 g brown sugar

  4. 1 cinnamon stick

  5. A few cloves

Instructions:

Combine ingredients: In a large glass jar, combine the pineapple chunks, water, sugar, and spices.

Ferment: Cover the jar with a cloth or paper towel and secure it with a rubber band. Let it ferment at room temperature for 2-3 days, or until it becomes bubbly and slightly acidic.

Strain and serve: Strain the tepache to remove the pineapple chunks. You can serve it immediately or store it in the refrigerator for later.
